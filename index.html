Peter Constantinou

Artist

Peter Constantinou attended Warren Wilson College in Asheville, NC where he graduated with a B.A. in fine art, with a concentration in photography. Peter is a film based portrait photographer often focusing on fashion subjects and creatives. He uses his knowledge of photography and passion for alternative processes to create images that capture the creative energy of the subject. Peter will often explore themes such as entropy and control by altering his film in unconventional methods to produce stunning images.
